Find your rhythm.

25|5 Classic Focus (duration: 2 hours)

The traditional way of getting stuff done. Focus on a task for 25 minutes then take a 5-minute break. Repeat for 4 times.

20|10 Moderate Focus (duration: 2 hours)

This is a less taxing way to work. Focus for 20 minutes then take a 10-minute break. Repeat for 4 times.

45|15 Power Focus (duration: 3 hours)

Sometimes you have to power through a lot of work. Focus for 45 minutes the take a 15-minute break. Repeat for 3 times.

The 5-min Break.

  • Go for a walk
  • Organize workspace
  • Stand up and stretch
  • Meditate / unplug
  • Check email
  • Prepare for the next task

The break is vital to letting your mind refresh and recover after intense focus. At the same time, it reminds your body to keep moving.

Everyone has a rhythm–find yours and stick to it.
