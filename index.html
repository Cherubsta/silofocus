How it works.

  1. Choose a task to work on, and press focus.
  2. After 25 minutes, SILO taps you on the wrist for a 5-minute break.
  3. After break, another tap nudges you back to work—for 4 cycles.

Taking breaks.

The short break is key to letting your mind refresh after a period of intense focus.

Taking your mind off of work for 5 minutes is like a breath of fresh air, a reminder to keep the body moving, or:

  • meditate / unplug
  • go for a walk
  • check news / email
  • organize workspace
  • stand up and stretch
  • prepare a new task
