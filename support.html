SILO Troubleshooting.

If you are having trouble with SILO please consider the following solutions:

  1. If the app is not showing up on your Apple Watch or in the Watch App on your iPhone, try power cycling your WATCH then your iPhone.

  2. For trouble with notifications: delete the SILO app from your iPhone, make sure it's off your WATCH, then reinstall from the App Store here.

  3. To force quit an app on WATCH: hold down the Dock button, when power-off menu appears, hold down the Crown button.

  4. Make sure you are running the latest versions of iOS, watchOS, and of course SILO.

  5. Be sure Notifications are Allowed: from iPhone go to Settings > SILO > Notifications > turn on allow notifications. From the Watch app, go to Notifications > SILO switch on. Also, scroll down and switch ON again to mirror alerts from iPhone.

  6. If your WATCH is loose on your wrist, it may accidentally lock (you may see a blue lock symbol on the top the of watch screen). When it's locked, notifications cannot go through.

  7. SILO will not work if iPhone/Watch is in "Do Not Disturb" mode (it will work in Airplane Mode).

  8. Consider increasing Haptic Strength: from WATCH go to Settings > Sounds & Haptics > increase Haptic Strength. Or turn on "Prominent Haptics" for an extra tap.

  9. If you are using Bluetooth headphones via WATCH during a session, it could suppress the haptics.

  10. And if those don't work: delete SILO app, then restart and/or restore WATCH and iPhone.

  11. If you still have difficulties, let me know: contact@silofocus.com
