How it works.

  1. Choose a task to work on, and press focus.
  2. After 25 minutes, SILO taps your wrist to take a 5-minute break.
  3. After break, another tap nudges you back to work—for 4 cycles.

Track work hours.

SILO simultaneously tracks your hours, while you focus. A time tracker designed to just work—there’s nothing you need to do besides press focus.

For those looking for a simpler time tracker without too many features, SILO has you covered.

Take breaks.

  • Go for a walk
  • Check news / email
  • Organize workspace
  • Stand up and stretch
  • Prepare a next task
